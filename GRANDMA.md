# Explaining My Project to My Grandma

This is a simple website that helps people decide what movie to watch.

When someone opens the website, they can type what kind of movie they feel like watching. For example, they can say something like "a funny movie" or "a sad love story".

After they press a button, the website thinks about their request and suggests a few movies that match what they want. It also gives a short explanation for each movie.

So instead of spending a long time trying to decide what to watch, the website helps them choose quickly.

Behind the scenes, the website uses an AI system that reads what the person writes and generates suggestions based on it. It is not using a fixed list so it will always be giving new movie recommendations depending on what the person asked.

In short, the website listens to what the person wants and helps them find a good movie to watch.

Sometimes the suggestions may not always be perfect because the website might only have a small amount of information coming from the person. Regardless, it still helps them get an idea of what to watch.
